Welcome to Sam's Sugar Shop

My name is Samantha, and I am a self-taught baker. While working towards my degree at CSUB, I needed something to help me decompress at the end of the day. I started following some bakers on Instagram, and over the years, I've taught myself how to bake by recreating recipes I've found online while adding my own twist.

At Sam's Sugar Shop, we offer both vegan and non-vegan options of all your favorite pastries and goodies. From cakes to cookies to breads and more, we're sure everyone in your family will find something they love on our menu!
